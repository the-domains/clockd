Looking To Fill A Job?

Why Are We Here?

The relationship most people have with work, especially hourly workers, is very unhealthy. Aren’t we meant for more than waiting for the phone to ring so we can pick up another few hours of time at $8 per hour? Clock’d is here with the audacity to question this relationship and seeks to create real solutions that enable people to chase their dreams.

So what are we going to do about it? The short version: create a digital ecosystem that fundamentally changes the way business and talent go about working with one another. Hiring sucks, employers seeking talent can’t stand the status quo (spending hundreds of dollars posting to a job board, weeks interviewing, losing time and money every step of the way), and no one looking for a job is really that fond of it either (when you need a job, you need a job). Small businesses are watching their profit margins shrink in the shadow of the ever more dominant juggernaut of Amazon, and wondering how they can keep the lights on. We are here to turn staffing pain into an advantage through real time human capital optimization.
